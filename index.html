How the impact adjustment works: B-Corp engagement scores below the midpoint (mission-aligned, low-harm, transparent work) produce a negative adjustment — a "mission discount" that lowers the financial margin threshold. Scores above the midpoint (mission tension, elevated human-rights or environmental risk, misleading claims) produce a positive adjustment — a "harm premium" that raises the hurdle rate. The B-Corp systematic score shifts the baseline: high portfolio-level impact risk widens both discounts and premiums. Use the manual override to set a conscious, documented impact adjustment for board-level decisions.
