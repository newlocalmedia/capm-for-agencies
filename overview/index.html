CAPM For Agencies

Overview

This project adapts CAPM into a shared decision framework for agency pricing. The main app is the interactive calculator, but the value of the model is the discipline around naming systematic risk, engagement risk, and impact tradeoffs before a deal is accepted.

The hybrid layer should be read as heuristic pricing governance, not as a statistically correct asset-pricing engine. Its practical utility is internal alignment, presales discipline, and postmortem calibration.

What It Does

Three Layers Of Use

  • Layer 1 scores portfolio-wide systematic conditions that shape the pricing environment across the whole book of business.
  • Layer 2 scores deal-level uncertainty, then compares the proposed deal margin against a required hurdle rate.
  • The B-Corp card adds impact-oriented governance factors so mission-aligned or harmful work is treated explicitly, not hand-waved.
How To Read It

Use The Math As Governance

The model is most useful when sales, solutions, delivery, and leadership are forced to use the same vocabulary for risk before committing. The numbers matter, but the governance effect matters more.
