CAPM for Agencies

Price Before You Plan

This project adapts CAPM into a shared decision framework for agency pricing. The main app is the interactive calculator, but the model is most useful when it forces sales, solutions, delivery, and leadership to name risk in the same language before a deal is accepted.

The hook is "price before you plan," but the intended meaning is narrower: price before delivery planning is committed, not before the solutions team has done enough technical assessment to judge complexity, client concerns, and whether implementation or discovery is the thing to sell.

The hybrid layer should be read as heuristic pricing governance, not as a statistically correct asset-pricing engine. Its practical utility is internal alignment, presales discipline, and postmortem calibration.

What It Does

Three Layers of Use

  • Layer 1 scores portfolio-wide systematic conditions that shape the pricing environment across the whole book of business.
  • Layer 2 scores deal-level uncertainty, then compares the proposed deal margin against a required hurdle rate.
  • The B-Corp card adds impact-oriented governance factors so mission-aligned or harmful work is treated explicitly, not hand-waved.
